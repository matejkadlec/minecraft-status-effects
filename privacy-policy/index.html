1. Overview

This website ("the Site") provides an interactive reference of Minecraft status effects. Your privacy matters; this document explains what limited data is (or is not) collected.

2. Data Controller

The Site is operated by Matěj Kadlec. Contact: Discord.

3. Data Collected

This Site does not use analytics, advertising trackers, or profiling cookies.

  • Local Storage: Only a single preference key (mcse-theme) is stored locally to remember your dark/light theme selection. This never leaves your device.
  • Server Logs: Standard, minimal web server logs (IP address, user agent, timestamp, requested resource) may exist transiently for security/diagnostics. They are not used to profile users.
  • No Account System: The Site does not offer registration, comments, or uploads.

4. Cookies

The Site sets no cookies. Consequently, a cookie consent banner is not required under EU ePrivacy/GDPR because no non-essential cookies or third‑party trackers are used.

5. Third‑Party Resources

  • Google Fonts: Fonts are requested from Google’s CDN. Your IP address and standard browser headers are sent to deliver the font files. Consider using a system font stack or self‑hosting if you want to avoid this external request entirely.
  • Remix Icon CDN: Icon font is fetched from jsDelivr CDN.

No other third‑party scripts (analytics, ads, social widgets) are embedded.

6. Legal Basis

For the very limited processing (serving static assets / fonts), the legal basis is legitimate interest in delivering the Site’s functionality.

7. Your Rights (EU/EEA)

You may request deletion of any personal data under control (effectively only server logs if retained) or additional clarification. Contact via the Discord link above.

8. Data Retention

Server logs (if retained) are typically rotated and deleted automatically within a short operational window (e.g., 7–30 days). Local Storage data persists on your device until you clear site data or toggle theme.

9. Security

The Site is static; no user‑submitted data is processed. Standard hosting provider safeguards apply.

10. International Transfers

Requests to Google Fonts and jsDelivr may involve infrastructure outside the EU. These providers commonly employ globally distributed CDNs.

11. Changes to this Policy

Material updates will change the “Last updated” date. Continued use indicates acceptance of any revised terms.

12. Contact

For privacy inquiries: reach out via Discord.
